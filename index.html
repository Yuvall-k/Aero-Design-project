About

Aquarius, a single motor propelled, mid-wing aircraft with tricycle landing gear designed to compete in SAE Aero Design East 2023. In compliance with our mission statement, we have fabricated a system capable of combating wildfires by safely and autonomously carrying water and components for a Ground Transport Vehicle (GTV) via a Powered Autonomous Delivery Aircraft (PADA).

The primary aircraft boasts an aerodynamic fuselage and a semi-tapered wing planform with split end wing tips.Its structure is reinforced from advanced materials such as Carbon Fiber (CF), Fiberglass (FG) and Nomex Honeycomb Core (HC).
